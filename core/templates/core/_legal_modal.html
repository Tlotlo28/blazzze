SOUTH AFRICAN LEGAL CONTEXT

For real-world context (not legal advice):

  • The Cannabis for Private Purposes Act (signed into law in 2024) governs personal cannabis use in South Africa.
  • The Drugs and Drug Trafficking Act, 1992 remains relevant for commercial scenarios outside the private-use framework.
  • Medical and commercial activity is separately regulated. License requirements apply.

The legal landscape continues to evolve. None of the above constitutes legal advice. Specific questions should go to a qualified South African attorney.
